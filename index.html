Who We Are

We are a passionate community of engineers, innovators, and hardware geeks at KUET. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical hardware design.

From designing custom PCBs and programming FPGAs to building autonomous robotics and IoT ecosystems, we aim to accelerate hardware innovation and give students hands-on experience in cutting-edge tech.
